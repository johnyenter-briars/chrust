Chrust: Chess + Rust <3

Try your hand at beating Rusty!

How to Play

You will always play as white and Rusty will always play as black.

Drag and drop your pieces to make moves. After you make a move, Rusty will make his move. Even if he doesn't immediately move his piece, give him time! He's thinking.

You will know who's move it currently is by checking under the header: "Current Move" up near the top

Refresh the browser or click the "Reset Board" button to start a new game.

Important to Note

  • Rusty will keep playing even after the game is over! Rusty doesn't know anything about how to win the game. He just wants your most valuable pieces!
  • There is no castling funtionality built into the game.
  • There will be some visual bugs if you try to make a move while Rusty is still thinking.
